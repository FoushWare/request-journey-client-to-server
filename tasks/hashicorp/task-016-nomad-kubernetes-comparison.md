## Theory Section

### Nomad vs Kubernetes

**Nomad Advantages:**
- Simpler to learn and operate
- Lighter weight
- Works with mixed workloads
- Good for small to medium teams

**Kubernetes Advantages:**
- Larger ecosystem
- More features
- Industry standard
- Better for complex applications

**When to Use Nomad:**
- Simpler orchestration needs
- Mixed workloads (containers + VMs)
- Smaller teams
- Want simplicity

**When to Use Kubernetes:**
- Complex microservices
- Need extensive ecosystem
- Large teams
- Industry standard required
